Interesting facts

Your dog is as smart as a two-year-old.

Did you know that dogs understand approximately 250 words or gestures? Very similar to what happens to a 2 year old child. That's why it's so common for dogs and toddlers to get along so well.

They can hear 4 times more than humans

Dogs have a highly developed sense of hearing, and this has to do with the frequencies of sounds and how their brains respond. They are able to hear sounds so 'low' that we are unable to detect them.

Physically, a one-year-old dog is equivalent to a 15-year-old human.

This, logically, is also going to depend on each breed... Generally, big dogs age faster than small dogs. There are some calculators that help you to more accurately know the age of your dog.

Dog...

The dog, called domestic dog or canine, and in some places colloquially called chucho, tuso, choco, among others, is a carnivorous mammal of the canid family, which is a species of the genus Canis.
